This voice library is trained alongside the following corpora:

- Millefeuille (https://utaufrance.com/millefeuille-diffsinger/)
- Tiny Italian Spanish Dataset (https://github.com/gnloop/Tiny-Italian-Spanish-Dataset)
- PJS Corpus (https://sites.google.com/site/shinnosuketakamichi/research-topics/pjs_corpus)
	- Labels by UtaUtaUtau, edited by tigermeat
- team.coda support data
- Small Parts of M4Singer (https://m4singer.github.io/)
- PrinttoTH Dataset
- BRAPA Private Dataset
